# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2023-05-16  
**Last Update:** 2024-03-07

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you visit or make use of the To-do List website (the "Website") operated by Adrian Środoń ("we", "us" or "our").

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy notice, or our practices with regards to your personal information, please contact us at [adrian.srodon@gmail.com](mailto:adrian.srodon@gmail.com).

## 1. Personal Information We Collect

**_In Short_**: we collect information that you provide to us.

We are collecting certain personal information when you visit or use our Website.  
This information include:

- OAuth2 profile information (first and last name, email address, avatar image etc.)
- Any other information you voluntarily provide to us. (e.g. creating new tasks, updating existing tasks, etc.)

### Automatically collected informations

**_In Short_**: we collect information about your IP address, browser and device characteristics (e.g. user agent).

When you are using our website, we are automatically collecting certain informations.
This informations does not reveal your specific identiy (like your name or contact information) but may include device and usage information, such as your IP address, browser and device characteristics, operating system, language preferences, referring URLs, device name, country, location, information about who and when you use our Website and other technical information.
This information is primarily needed to maintain the security and operation of our Website, and for our internal analytics and reporting purposes (logs).

Logs are automatically retained by our hosting provider - [Vercel](https://vercel.com), and later shared to [Axiom](https://axiom.co) to further analyze the traffic and debug operating issues.

### Analytics

We are using self-hosted Umami Analytics instance.  
It is hosted on [Google Cloud](https://cloud.google.com) using Compute Engine (us-east-1-b region - South Carolina, USA).

We do **not collect** any personal or identifiable data, therefore no cookie / GDPR banner is required.  
All of the data is aggregated data only and it has no personal information.

If you would like to learn more about data policy, please check [Umami website](https://umami.is/features).

## 2. Use of Personal Information

**_In Short_**: we process your information for purposes based on legitimate business interesets with compilance with our legal obligations.

We use the personal information collected to:

- Provide and improve our services
- Respond to your inquiries

3rd party services we are using:

We will retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information.

By using our Website, you agree to the collection and use of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy.
